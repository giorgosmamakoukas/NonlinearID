Information for temporal textures example.

Dependencies:

Yalmip
Use: setting up SDP programs for LREE, Lacy & Bernstein and Constraint Generation.
Available: https://yalmip.github.io/

Mosek
Use: solver for SDP programs.
Note: other solvers may be used with simple modifications to the code.
Available: https://www.mosek.com/

Instructions:

Run 'temporal_textures_example.m' to generate the results in Section V.C of the paper.
